✦ I hiked this 🚂 SBB Direct Moderate Bernese Oberland

Oeschinensee Loop, Kandersteg

A glacial lake surrounded by sheer limestone walls reaching over 2,000 metres. One of the most beautiful half-days I have spent in the Bernese Oberland. The colour of the water needs to be seen to be believed.

11 km distance ↑ 750m ascent 4–5h duration

✦ I hiked this 🚂 SBB Direct Easy Via Jacobi

Via Jacobi Stage 13, Schwarzenburg to Fribourg

22 km across the Bernese Midland — hilly fields, forest, streams and villages — finishing in one of Switzerland's finest medieval cities. Hiked in heavy rain and snow. Start and end both train stations.

~22 km distance ↑ 664m ascent 4.5–6h duration

✦ I hiked this 🚂 SBB Direct Easy ❄️ Snowshoe Bernese Oberland

Spittelmatten Snowshoe Trail, Sunnbüel

A 5 km winter loop through the Sunnbüel valley near Kandersteg. Peaks, forest, a frozen waterfall and ice climbers at the far end. One of the best winter half-days in the Bernese Oberland.

5 km distance ↑ 260m ascent 2.5–3h duration
